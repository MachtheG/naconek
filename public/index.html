Who We Are

Overview & Strategic Context

"Driving technical solutions to achieve parity in primary literacy indicators across marginalized settings."

The National Council for Nomadic Education in Kenya (NACONEK) operates as a Semi-Autonomous Government Agency (SAGA) registered within the Ministry of Education. Established through formal statutory decree under the Basic Education Act of 2013, our target mandate centers on engineering structural solutions that ensure equitable, high-quality basic education.

Our operational theater includes twenty-four Arid and Semi-Arid counties, pocket communities within agricultural zones, and temporary settlement tracks. Through specialized logistics mapping and resource aggregation, we mitigate structural barriers to class access.

Universal School Health, Nutrition & Meals

Provides a crucial safety net across drought-prone segments. Ensuring programmatic dry food allocations stabilizes classroom retention indexes, counteracts local migration shocks, and preserves cognitive performance capacity.

Integrated Pedagogy

Duksi & Madrasa Integration

Integrating secular literacy targets directly into traditional structures.

By coordinating with traditional learning institutions, NACONEK injects primary literacy frameworks, basic mathematics modules, and linguistic assets directly into existing structural timetables.

This expands standard educational pathways for thousands of children without causing cultural friction or local community fragmentation.
